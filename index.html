About Me

My name is Samir and I have been a DJ with heart and soul for over 25 years now.

I started my career as a teenager DJing private parties at high school and university in Egypt. Shortly after I had the opportunity to work for one of the biggest "Sound & Light Company" in Cairo and learned the handling in the field of "Sound & Light concept" through the event department, which booked me regularly as a DJ at hotels in Sharm el Sheikh. Further regular bookings in renowned clubs followed and my career continued to develop on a professional path.

My talent for the right choice of music was more and more in demand for private events, such as weddings, birthdays, corporate events or other private events. My passion for music and my desire to entertain the audience gave me an extraordinary journey through the music industry.

Today I have an excellent music set with a wide range of sound styles for the perfect mood on the dance floor, a fast growing fan base and I am also well versed in the technical field. With my years of experience, I can provide you with the best musical moments and skillfully guide the audience through your event.

Currently I am working as a resident DJ in a renowned club in the city of Zurich and am regularly booked for parties at events. Contact me today for a non-binding offer.
